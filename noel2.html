City Scandal

Philip wakes up and heads off to the factory His breaks are short and quite unsatisfactory His hands are tough, tough to the touch His days are long and his hours are too much Philip works so he can help and provide But phillip knows his toils are unjustified Along with his starvation and his days of frustration Philip is a slave to the american corporation The coins in his pockets are barely a profit This life of hardship is not something he wanted His family came to find better lives But his family was bribed by the american lie The american dream is just propaganda As it turns out the city life is all but a scandall
